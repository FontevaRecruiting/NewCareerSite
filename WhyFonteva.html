What is Life Like at Fonteva?

Fontevans are an educated bunch. Over 75% of us have advanced degrees and/or professional certifications—including Salesforce, of course. And Fontevans love to continue to learn and grow. Fontevans—both current and alumni—say they learn and accomplish more here in a week than they had in a whole year in their previous roles.

As Fonteva grows at a rapid pace, our aim is for Fontevans to grow their careers at equal speed. Just one example is a Fontevan who was hired out of his Master’s program just less than 3 years ago, and he is now an expatriate Delivery Manager in our London office.

Describe the Culture at Fonteva

Fonteva is comprised of a broad range of multifaceted individuals who contribute to our company’s vibrant, unique culture. Jerry, our CEO, likes to call us a “Silicon Valley start-up in Arlington;” this captures our environment of innovation and collaboration—and admittedly, sometimes chaos—to a T. We have moved beyond the start-up to a “growth company” stage, and we hope will always be, true of our culture.

Fontevans are Fun, Friendly and Interesting!

Visitors, jobseekers and new employees always comment on Fonteva’s friendliness. With the need for so much collaboration and interdependence, we do strive to keep a spirit of camaraderie and respect, even with our differences and disagreements.

Although we are intense, we also like to have fun—with monthly happy hours, summer and winter events, birthday celebrations—anything that includes good food! And speaking of food, another Fonteva favorite is our fully stocked kitchen.

Included in our fun are Fontevans’ interests outside of work—mostly in the areas of music and travel. One of our Delivery Directors is a lead singer in a few bands. It’s not uncommon to find our CTO and VP of Customers for Life jamming on their guitars together in their offices (after 5, of course). With our diverse population, most Fontevans are well-travelled--the current frontrunner is our HR Director who’s travelled to 46 countries… and counting

What about Diversity?

Diversity is the rule at Fonteva—we are diverse is many ways. This often means race/nationality, and our heritage spans the globe, with Asian, African American, Hispanic and European. Fontevans go back “home” to California, Delaware, Illinois, Tennessee…we’re working on Fontevans from all 50 states!

Fontevans are also single, married, LGBTQ, married with children, unmarried with children, partnered and consciously uncoupled. We are Millennials, Gen X and Boomers. We are Liberal and Conservative; Christian, Jewish, Muslim, Buddhist, Hindu, Agnostic & Atheist. And we have lovers of Ed Sheeran, Jay Z, Rush, Vince Gill and Placido Domingo, to name a few!

Do you have a Dress Code?

Like our diverse population, we have a diverse but ‘business casual’ dress code. Some Fontevans are walking billboards & should be on the cover of GQ or Vogue. Others’ fashion icons are Mark Zuckerberg and Steve Jobs. And we all have an ample supply of Fonteva swag to wear. In the summer, we tend to dress down more, and in the winter, we bundle up (our building has a mind of its own: on one end, it’s Sub-Saharan Africa; on the other, it’s Antarctica). When we have special visitors, we’re forced (or get the privilege) to dress up.
