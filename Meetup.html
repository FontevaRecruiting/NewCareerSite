About

Fonteva develops innovative solutions for non-profits, associations, colleges and universities using the Salesforce platform. We are on the forward edge of association management software and push the limits of cloud-based AMS solutions.

Why does Fonteva host this Meetup group? Because we enjoy interacting with the Salesforce development community. Our events are great opportunities to learn new techniques, methods, frameworks or just "talk tech" with industry leaders. Come join us and see why Fonteva is the industry leader in cloud-based association management software.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the Fonteva Meetup Group?

The Meetup Group was designed to provide technologists and professionals with the opportunity to learn more about Fonteva in a fun, relaxed atmosphere. Fonteva is known for our best-in-breed AMS products, but that does not tell the whole story. We want to provide technologists with the opportunity to learn why working at Fonteva is a great opportunity; both professionally and personally. We frequently have executives, vice presidents and special guests attend our networking events. Come out and meet our diverse team!

How do the Meetups work?

It starts by registering through our Meetup page. This is the best place to learn more about upcoming Meetup dates, topics, guest speakers and all other relevant information. You can sign up to attend our events, send messages to group members, Fonteva administrators or provide feedback on events.

We host all events onsite at our offices in Arlington, VA. The Meetup group will typically have a theme, light refreshments, and alcoholic and non-alcoholic beverages.

What if I don't code?

No problem! Not everyone is a programmer! We frequently meet professionals in accounting, finance, sales, business development, marketing, project management and other disciplines.

Who can attend?

Our Meetup Group is open to any technical or working professional seeking to learn more about Fonteva. We ask that anyone who is interested register through our Meetup page, but other than that there are very few requirements.

What if I am not ready to leave my current job?

No problem! We want to meet any technologist or working professional who is interested in Fonteva. People attend our Meetup for various reasons. Individuals attend to learn more about opportunities, others to learn about the company and some visit just to hang out with professionals in a similar industry. No matter your reason, all are welcome to attend. In fact, why not bring a friend or colleague?

What topics do you cover at the Meetups?

Anything and everything! That's the beauty of our group; no Meetup is ever the same. We typically cover topics that are relevant to our industry. Some sample topics include Salesforce Development, Web 2.0 technologies, DevOps, system engineering, application architecture, sales, marketing and more!

What should I bring?

Nothing, just bring yourself! Guests are always welcome to bring friends, colleagues and co-workers.

How long does the Meetup Last?

The Meetup group is held at our headquarters in Arlington, VA. Events typically last approximately 1.5 to 2 hours.

Are there any rules?

Be respectful of all people and property, act professional and have fun!

I have more questions!

Sure! Send us an email and we'll respond as quickly as possible. We look forward to hearing from you! Email , Twitter , or Facebook.
